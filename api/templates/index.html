ABOUT

The Internet Reality Project is an educational resource that aims to equip users with the critical skills necessary to identify fake news and instill confidence in their ability to apply these skills with ease in their everyday lives.

As an industry, digital technologies have undeniably played a significant role in the influx of fake news as of recent years. And as such I believe that it is in part the responsibility of digital technology creators to address this issue as a unified front. This website serves as a mock up for a potential practical, engaging, and acsessible educational resource the industry could develop.



OUR MISSION

The Internet Reality Project is an unbiased and controlled environment for education surrounding the issue of Fake News. Particularly in recent years this issue has gone from a niche topic to a global debate that politicatial factoctions weponise and that the public fear. Never has humanity been more polorised in opinion, and a lot of this is down to the influence of Fake News. The Intnernet Reality Project aims to bridge the divide and foster the skills needed to identify fact from fiction on the internet. The ability to determine truth from lies should not be an exclusive one. This website is an accessible place where you can educate yourself and go forth as a more responsible conscious internet user.
